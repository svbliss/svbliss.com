Tar (Segment Still Life)
Collaborative Feature | HD Cannon 5D/7D

Credits
Written and Directed by Sarah-Violet Bliss
Produced by RabbitBandini Productions via Vince Jolivette, Shruti Ganguli
Director of Photography Pedro Gomez Millan
Featuring James Franco, Jordan March, Danika Yarosh

Synopsis
Poet CK Williams relives the memory of an idyllic afternoon with a new friend and briefly, eternally falls in love. A moment encapsulated, if not trapped, in a moment in time. Based on the poem "Still Life" from Williams' collection of poems, Tar.

Tar was shot on location in and surrounding Detroit Michigan.
